fonts folder is for font files

images are for images and multi-image folders

music is for music

scripts is for .gd files outside the main tscn
(example: game.gd stays next to game.tscn while other_game_funtion.gd gets put in the scripts folder)

sfx is for sound effects

shaders is for the .gdshader files

memes folder is for assets that are not serious like vault assets, joke sfx, etc
